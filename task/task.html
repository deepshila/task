A natural product as a high-performance material?
Basalt fibers and the diverse products that are made from them create what seems incompatible at first glance.

Above all, basalt products are suitable as a steel substitute in the construction industry . Over three times stronger than steel reinforcements with the same diameter and three times lighter. Basalt does not corrode , unlike steel.
